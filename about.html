About Us

Tanya

Tanya Archer started hairdressing in 2001. She trained extensively at Toni & Guy Salons, specializing in color. She excels at many different techniques, including technicolor shades and beautiful highlights. She also does Brazilian Blowouts to perfection. Tanya will make sure you love your color before you leave her chair. Her infectious laughter is one of the things that makes the salon so special. She has a passion for people and hairdressing. By appointment only.

Cherie

Cherie Wiggins started hairdressing in 2001, specializing in color. She trained at the prestigious Toni & Guy Academy, after graduating moving on to Toni & Guy Salons, where she received further education. She never fails to produce the coveted perfect blonde. She specializes in classic fine highlighting which results in a more natural, flowing look. She also does specialty techniques. Cherie has a passion for gorgeous, seamless color. By appointment only.

Heather

Heather Wilhelm has been cutting and styling hair since 1993. She trained extensively with Toni & Guy Hairdressing, later becoming an Artistic Director and educator for the salon. Specializing in cutting and styling, she takes into account facial structure, lifestyle, and ease of styling for each particular client. Hair is a family affair. She follows in the footsteps of her grandmother, Alma, who was also a hairdresser for many years. Heather loves interacting with people and finding the perfect look to suit an individual. By appointment only.
